About Us

Sourcerer is a professional services and managed services firm founded by a senior leader in talent acquisition, recruitment, and go-to-market strategy. We help organisations operate efficiently, scale responsibly, and execute with clarity.

We work as an extension of our clients’ teams, providing structured support across sourcing, operations, and ongoing managed service engagements.

How We Work

Sourcerer is industry agnostic. We support organisations across a wide range of sectors, focusing on how teams operate rather than the industry they belong to.

Our work centres on building structured, reliable, and scalable hiring and operational systems that respect privacy, context, and human judgement. These principles apply universally regardless of domain.

We believe good operational discipline, responsible use of technology, and clear decision making are not industry specific concerns. They are fundamentals.

Our Approach

We advocate for the responsible and ethical use of AI in hiring and talent operations. Automation should enhance human decision-making not replace judgment, context, or accountability.

Our work prioritises transparency, explainability, and bias awareness across all AI-assisted workflows.

Privacy & Data Responsibility

We are privacy-first by design. We collect only what is necessary, retain data minimally, and avoid unnecessary exposure of client or candidate information.

No data scraping, no resale, no dark patterns. Operational security and trust are non-negotiable.

We review all enquiries carefully. If your request is relevant, we’ll be in touch. We work selectively with a limited number of clients.
